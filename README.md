# AgriShield – AI Powered Crop Disease Detection System

## Description
AgriShield is an AI-powered smart agriculture system designed to monitor crop health and detect plant diseases at an early stage. The system analyzes crop images and identifies visible symptoms such as leaf discoloration, spots, and unusual patterns. Based on the detected disease, AgriShield recommends suitable organic fertilizers and natural treatments that are easily available in the farmer’s local area. The goal of this system is to support sustainable farming practices, reduce chemical fertilizer usage, and improve crop yield.

## Problem Statement
Farmers often struggle to identify crop diseases during the early stages. Due to lack of proper knowledge and monitoring tools, diseases spread quickly and reduce crop productivity. Farmers usually depend on chemical fertilizers and pesticides without proper guidance, which can damage soil health and increase farming costs. Therefore, there is a need for a smart system that can detect crop diseases early and guide farmers with effective organic solutions.

## Proposed Solution
AgriShield uses Artificial Intelligence and computer vision techniques to analyze crop images and detect plant diseases automatically. Cameras or monitoring devices capture crop images, and the AI model processes these images to identify disease symptoms. Once a disease is detected, the system provides recommendations for organic fertilizers and natural treatments that farmers can easily access in their local environment.

## System Architecture
1. Crop images are captured using cameras or monitoring devices.
2. Images are sent to the AI processing system.
3. The AI model analyzes the image to detect disease symptoms.
4. The system identifies the disease using a crop disease database.
5. Organic fertilizer and treatment recommendations are generated.
6. Results are provided to farmers or agriculture monitoring centers.
